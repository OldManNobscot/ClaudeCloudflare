Jocelyne's Major List

Majors and career outcomes across Curry, Dean, FSU, MassBay, Mitchell, and UNE — filtered by fit and school.

Major School(s) What it's about What you can do with it Fit
Strong fit — storytelling, media, creativity, culture, writing
Communication / Media and Communication Arts CurryDeanFSU How people share ideas through media, writing, and storytelling. Covers journalism, social media, public relations, and how cultural messages get made and spread. Social media manager, content writer, communications coordinator, PR assistant, journalist, marketing associate, media producer, nonprofit communications staff. Strong fit
Digital Communication Mitchell Content creation, media storytelling, and digital platforms. You study how media shapes culture and how to produce your own. Digital content creator, social media strategist, podcast or video producer, community manager, brand communications associate, copywriter. Strong fit
English CurryFSU Reading, writing, and understanding how stories work. You analyze literature, film, and culture while developing strong writing skills. Writer, editor, communications staff, content strategist, teacher (with licensure), publishing assistant, grant writer, technical writer, UX writer. Strong fit
Creative Writing UNE Fiction, essays, and screenplays in small workshop settings. Everyone reads and responds to each other's work throughout the program. Author, screenwriter, content writer, copywriter, editor, communications professional, writing instructor. Writing skills transfer into almost any career. Strong fit
Fashion Design and Retailing / Apparel and Textile Science FSU Combines the creative side of fashion with how clothing gets designed, produced, and sold. Hands-on and visual throughout. Fashion buyer, retail merchandiser, visual merchandiser, fashion stylist, product developer, apparel designer, trend analyst, fashion marketing coordinator. Strong fit
Communication Arts FSU FSU's blend of visual communication, media production, and storytelling. More hands-on and design-oriented than a traditional communications major. Graphic designer, visual content producer, media coordinator, UX/UI designer, brand designer, advertising associate, digital media specialist. Strong fit
Communication (A.A.) MassBay Two-year associate degree covering the basics of media, writing, and communication. Designed to transfer to a four-year program. Entry-level communications or media assistant roles, or transfer pathway to communications, English, or media studies at a four-year school. Strong fit
English (A.A.) MassBay Two-year degree in reading, writing, and literature. Transfers well to four-year programs in English, communications, or education. Transfer pathway to a four-year English or humanities degree. Entry-level writing, tutoring, or editorial assistant roles while continuing your education. Strong fit
Liberal Arts (A.A.) MassBay A broad foundation in writing, humanities, and social sciences. A flexible starting point that transfers easily to a four-year school. Primarily a transfer degree — it opens the door to almost any four-year major. Career outcomes depend on the direction taken after transfer. Strong fit
Art (Studio Art) FSUUNE Hands-on art making — drawing, painting, graphic arts, and visual design. Project-based learning throughout. Graphic designer, illustrator, art educator, gallery assistant, visual content creator, set or prop designer, freelance artist, art director (with experience). Strong fit

Arts Leadership Dean How the arts are managed, funded, and brought to communities. Covers nonprofit arts organizations, event planning, and cultural programming. Arts administrator, nonprofit program coordinator, event producer, development associate, cultural center manager, community arts educator, gallery or venue manager. Strong fit
Theatre (Acting or Production/Design) Dean Performance, directing, and production design. Dean is nationally recognized for its performing arts programs. Actor, stage manager, production designer, props or costume coordinator, arts educator, community theater director, entertainment production assistant. Strong fit
History FSUUNE How people, cultures, and societies have changed over time. Reading and writing focused. Jocelyne's World History Honors grade suggests this is a real strength area. Museum educator, archivist, researcher, journalist, policy analyst, teacher (with licensure), nonprofit program staff. Graduate school opens doors in law, public policy, or academic history. Strong fit
Global Studies / International Studies FSUUNE How cultures and societies interact across the world — through history, media, politics, and trade. Writing and reading intensive. Nonprofit or NGO program staff, international relations coordinator, cultural affairs associate, journalist, government or policy work. Graduate study in international affairs or law common. Strong fit

Transfer pathway to four-year psychology, sociology, counseling, or social work programs. Entry-level human services work while completing the degree. Possible fit

Public Health FSUUNE How communities stay healthy — health education, policy, and prevention. Less clinical than nursing. People-centered and writing-friendly.
